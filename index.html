Hello & Welcome!

I'm Abdallah Alsammani, an interdisciplinary mathematician and data scientist who translates complex problems into actionable insights. With over a decade of teaching experience and a research portfolio spanning biology, medicine, economics, and technology, I bridge the gap between theoretical rigor and real-world application. My expertise lies in mathematical modeling, applied statistics, and machine learning — tools I use to tackle questions that matter. Whether developing quantitative frameworks for biological systems or designing statistical solutions for emerging challenges, I thrive at the intersection of disciplines where creative problem-solving drives innovation. I'm committed to advancing knowledge through collaborative research and teaching that empower others to think critically and work confidently with data.
