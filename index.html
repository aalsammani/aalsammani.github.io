Abdallah Alsammani, Ph.D.

Assistant Professor of Mathematics and Data Science

Department of Mathematical Sciences

Delaware State University

Dover, Delaware 19901, USA

I am an applied mathematician and data scientist whose research develops mathematical models and computational methods, including machine learning and deep learning, for problems in public health, clinical medicine, and computational neuroscience. My work integrates dynamical systems (ODEs and SDEs), optimal control theory, and statistical learning to study infectious disease transmission, build predictive models from clinical and neural data (including intracranial EEG), and develop interpretable, reliable AI tools for biomedical decision support.
