Research

Research Interests

My research integrates computational mathematics, data science, and machine learning to tackle complex, real-world challenges. I focus on systems biology, medical imaging, computational neuroscience, epidemiology, public health, environmental science, and ecology. I seek collaborations that bridge theoretical models and empirical data, facilitating new tools, algorithms, and analytical methods for advancing knowledge in interdisciplinary fields.
