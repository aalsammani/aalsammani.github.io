Research Interests

My research integrates computational mathematics, data science, and machine learning to tackle complex, real-world challenges. I focus on systems biology, medical imaging, computational neuroscience, epidemiology, public health, environmental science, and ecology. I seek collaborations that bridge theoretical models with empirical data, creating new tools, algorithms, and analytical methods for interdisciplinary fields.
